ABOUT REMITMAS

RemitMas is the first online money transfer service that allows you to save for your family members in Latin America.

RemitMas uses innovative and secure technology to let you direct your money into savings accounts for education, healthcare, and housing for the future of your family from any device at any time.

RemitMas also offers you an interactive Savings Toolkit with financial education tutorials that let you earn points and win instant rewards!

THE TEAM

RemitMas’ vision is to transform remittances into vehicles for social change. RemitMas is a social venture, launched by Columbia University Students who want to create new opportunities for Latinos living abroad to send money home and better plan for their family's finances.

The RemitMas team comprises of highly qualified specialists in remittances services, banking and technology. Our teams are based in New York City and Bogota. RemitMas partners with experts in the money transfer ecosystem in Latin America.
